Lasagna

Description

Lasagna with Meat Sauce is a delightful Italian recipe that can be prepared for treating someone special. Loaded with cheese, this lasagna recipe might seem difficult at first with expert cooking skills.

much to your surprise, the more hard work you will use for making this amazing dish, the more fruitful it will be. This lasagna recipe uses five types of cheese and two types of meat. But, you can replace the pork meat if you do not have it as the dish will turn out well even without it. If you have to serve this dish in a party, make the preparations well in advance as it is an elaborate dish and might take some time to cook.

Ingredients

  • 1 kilograms ground mutton
  • 2 cup tomato ketchup
  • 2 clove minced garlic
  • 750 gm pork sausages
  • 3 tablespoon minced parsley
  • 8 ounce ricotta cheese
  • 9 sliced & slit lasagne sheets
  • 3 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pork and mutton meat
  2. Prepare the meat sauce
  3. Mix all the cheese with eggs
  4. Make the layering using cheese, lasagne sheet and meat sauce
  5. Bake the lasagna for 50 minutes at 375 degrees Fahrenheit
